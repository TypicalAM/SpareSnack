SpareSnackDayRecipes

Good diets, just for you.

This application is designed to make planning your meals easier and more conventient. It has a system of keeping your days and diets in place, for you to use them later in a different time. 

The purpose of this app

Why was this app created and what features does it have

Keep track

Your meals are kept in your days, for you to use them later and form your unique day plans which you can use to learn to follow a schedule

Save time

This app allows you to save days, or even sequences of days in a diet - you can use them on your own or even share them with the world.

Imagination

Let go of artistic boundaries and make your plan creative, you can set goals for yourself to work towards being a better person overall!
